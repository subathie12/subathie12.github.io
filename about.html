FASHION

Fashion & Personal Expression

Fashion has always been more than clothes for me — it’s been a form of identity, confidence, and creativity. It’s the first space where I learned how colours, structure, and detail can completely change how something feels. Fashion helped me explore who I am, express my emotions, and build visual storytelling long before I ever picked up a camera.

A lot of my creative direction, editing style, and aesthetic choices come from the way I learned to see outfits: balance, contrast, texture, personality. It shaped the way I think as a designer and a photographer.
